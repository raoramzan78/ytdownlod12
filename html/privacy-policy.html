Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy while using our YouTube Thumbnail Downloader

Our Privacy Commitment

At YouTube Thumbnail Downloader, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service.

Our tool is designed with privacy in mind, and we've taken steps to ensure that your personal information remains secure while using our service.

Information We Don't Collect

We believe in minimizing data collection. Here's what we do not collect:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal identifiers.
  • Account Information: No accounts or registration are required to use our service.
  • YouTube Videos: We do not download, store, or process the actual YouTube videos, only the publicly available thumbnails.
  • Search History: We do not store a history of the YouTube URLs you've entered.
  • Payment Information: Our service is completely free, so no payment details are ever collected.

Information We Process

To provide our service, we do process certain information:

  • YouTube URLs: When you enter a YouTube URL, we process it to extract the video ID needed to fetch the thumbnail.
  • Temporary Processing: The URL you enter is processed in your browser and is not stored on our servers after the thumbnails are fetched.
  • Standard Log Data: Our web servers automatically log standard information such as IP addresses and browser types for security and performance monitoring purposes. This data is anonymized and regularly purged.

How We Use Information

The limited information we process is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the YouTube thumbnail downloading service
  • Maintain and improve the performance and security of our website
  • Analyze aggregated, anonymous usage patterns to improve our service

Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses minimal cookies and local storage:

  • Functional Storage: We use local storage to remember your theme preference (light or dark mode).
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our service interacts with the following third parties:

  • YouTube: We access publicly available thumbnail images from YouTube's servers. This is done in accordance with YouTube's Terms of Service.
  • Hosting Provider: Our website is hosted on secure servers that maintain their own privacy and security measures.

We do not share any user information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

YouTube API Services

Our service accesses YouTube thumbnails through publicly available URLs and does not use the YouTube API. However, we respect YouTube's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

For information about Google's privacy policy, please visit Google Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
